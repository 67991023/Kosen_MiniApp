Welcome to KOSEN-KMITL

About the Program

The KOSEN-KMITL program is a collaboration between King Mongkut's Institute of Technology Ladkrabang (KMITL) and the Japanese KOSEN (National Institute of Technology) system. This innovative educational approach combines theoretical knowledge with practical skills to produce highly competent engineers.

Program Overview

KOSEN-KMITL offers a comprehensive 5-year engineering education program designed to develop students' technical expertise and problem-solving abilities from an early age. Our curriculum emphasizes hands-on learning, industry collaboration, and international standards.

Key Features

  • 5-year integrated engineering program
  • Hands-on practical training from year 1
  • International collaboration with Japanese KOSEN institutes
  • Industry partnerships and internships
  • Modern facilities and laboratories
